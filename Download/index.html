About SonPay

SonPay is a comprehensive digital payment application developed by Son LLC. It supports QR payments, NFC payments, shared and group payments, direct loan requests from the system, balance top-up via Binance, bill payments, gift card purchases, and a rewards system where users earn money from watching ads. SonPay also provides a physical SonPay Card with cashback rewards for new users.
